Pet Realocation

Pet Relocation Services in India—Safe, Secure & Stress-Free with NextGen

Moving to a new home is hard enough. But when you have a pet to move with you, the stress becomes double. Your dog, cat, bird, or any other animal is not just a pet, but it’s a part of your family. At NextGen Safe N Secure, we don’t treat pets as cargo. We handle each relocation with care and responsibility. Whether you’re moving within the city or interstate, we make sure your pet arrives safely, comfortably, and without trauma.

Paperwork Required for Pet Transportation in India

Just like in vehicle transportation, you need some essential documents to transport your pet in India. Here’s what you must arrange:

1. Health Certificate from a Vet

Must state that the pet is healthy and fit to travel. Required for all transport modes

2. Vaccination Record

Mandatory proof of rabies vaccination Other vaccines as per breed/type (we’ll guide you).

3. Carrier or Crate

Pets must be transported in a crate suitable for their size. You can bring your own crate or rent or buy one from us.All crates follow safety standards (non-metallic, chew-proof, ventilated). Any questions? Call us for a step-by-step guide.

Our Process—What Happens After You Call Us?

1
Consultation:

You call. We ask a few basic questions—pet type, weight, location, destination, and timeline.

2
Planning

We figure out the safest and fastest method—rail, road, or air.

3
Preparation

We help you in gathering all the required documents, picking the right crate, and setting a travel date.

4
Pickup & Relocation

We come to your door, pick up your pet with care, and start the journey.

5
Tracking & Delivery

We keep you updated. At the destination, we hand over your pet with proper identification and care instructions.

Final Thoughts

We understand how difficult it can be to hand over your pet to someone else. However, we have been doing this for years and know exactly how to make the process easy and stress-free for your furry friend.
