What is Car Transport?

Car transportation is the process of relocating your car from location A to B. It’s commonly used for relocation and moves, either by individuals shipping one or two cars or businesses shipping multiple cars at once. Whether you’re shipping within a state or interstate, choosing the right mode of transportation can save you money while ensuring fast shipping.

There are two main options for car transportation: Open and Enclosed Transportation.  In open car transportation, your car is exposed to the elements, which can cause damage from weather, dust, and debris. Enclosed car transportation, It involves putting your car in a container that protects it from these weather, dust and debris. This option comes with an additional cost, usually around 20-35% more than open transport.

When You choose a car transportation company, it’s important to select a professional car transporter who can help you find the right option based on your needs. For instance, if you’re transporting your everyday car, open car transport is often the best choice. It’s more affordable and is used in almost 95% of auto transport cases.

Factors that can affect the cost of car shipping

There are various factors that influence the cost of car shipping in India.

Mode of transport:

Open transport is 20-30% cheaper than enclosed transport, but it offers less protection. In open transport, your car is exposed to external entities like weather, dust, and debris, while enclosed transport protects your car from these.

Distance

The longer the distance, the higher will be the price for transportation. Terrains also plays a major role in determining the cost; for example, transporting a car to Srinagar is more costly because they have less rail infrastructure and deadly commuting routes.

Shipping Methods: 

Terminal to Terminal: This is an affordable method for transporting your car, but it’s a bit unsafe and less flexible as you’ll need to visit specific terminal points for pick-up and drop-off.

Door-to-door shipping 

It offers convenience, as the vehicle will be picked up and dropped off at your chosen location. However, it comes with a premium cost, which may vary depending on the drop-off and pickup locations.
