Privacy Policy – NextGen Safe N Secure

Overview

Your privacy is very important to us. At NextGen Safe N Secure, we take your data seriously — and we handle it with care. In our company NextGen Safe N Secure’s policies, we’ve outlined what information we’ll collect, how we’ll collect it, and why we’ll collect it. If you feel unsafe at any point, you’re free to not use our services. We collect personal info like your name, contact number, email, and address only when it’s necessary — like when you're requesting a quote or booking a service. We might also use public info (like your business card or social media links) to make our service smoother and faster for you. This policy is a part of the NextGen Safe N Secure User Agreement and applies to all users of our website and services.

What We Collect

In our company NextGen Safe N Secure’s policies, we’ve outlined what information we’ll collect, how we’ll collect it, and why we’ll collect it. If you feel unsafe at any point, you’re free to not use our services. You can visit our website without sharing your personal info. But if you're using our services — like requesting transport or tracking your delivery — we’ll ask for:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email
  • Pickup and drop location
  • ID proof (for verification)
  • Payment info (if applicable)

We also collect system data like your IP address and device info for basic security and performance tracking. Once you fill out a form or request a service, you’re no longer anonymous to us.

Methods We Use:

  • GPS tracking: It is optional. If you give us permission, we’ll track you. Otherwise, we won’t
  • Background tracking: When you’re active on our site, we track you.
  • You’re in control: You can turn off location sharing anytime in your phone settings

Data Usage & Why We Use It

We use your data strictly for:

  • Quoting and delivering our services
  • Avoiding fake leads and time-wasters
  • Contacting you if needed
  • Marketing our own logistics services (no spam, no reselling)
  • Improving your user experience

Note: We don’t share your personal data with third parties for ads or promotions

For User Verification

We verify IDs to filter out fake bookings and ensure safety. We may ask for:

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhar
  • Driver’s License
  • Vehicle Registration
  • Insurance
  • Pollution Certificate

For Service Providers

Packers & movers or transport vendors registering with us must provide:

  • Aadhar
  • PAN Card
  • GST Details
  • Bank Account
  • Udyam/Udyog Registration
  • Transport Permit

This helps us eliminate junk leads and verify businesses before letting them onboard.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely and used only to run our operations. We use encryption, secured forms, and up-to-date safety tools. Phone numbers may be shared between customer and vendor only for service coordination.

Policy Updates

We may perform minor or significant changes from time to time. So, check our privacy policies whenever you are using our services. 

Final Word

We’re not in the business of selling your data, so you can rest assured. We prioritise your trust and take it very seriously. We’re committed to moving things safely, securely, and transparently. Honesty is our best policy
