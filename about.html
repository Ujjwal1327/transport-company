About us

Overview

NextGen Safe N Secure Packers & Movers and Car Transport Company has been shipping cars and goods since 2016, with nine years of experience. We employ the most advanced techniques to transport your vehicles and help in office shifting, household shifting, bike transport and pet transport across India. Our name, NextGen, reflects the industry-leading advancements we’ve made and our commitment to continuous improvement.

We prioritize safety, security, and honest pricing for all our clients, along with ensuring the best customer experience and support. We offer a range of services, including car transportation, packing and moving, household shifting, office shifting, Pet relocation and shipping any other goods that need to be moved, such as machines, equipment, Spare parts or food items.
